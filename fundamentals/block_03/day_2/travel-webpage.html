Travel to Japan

Japan is a great place to visit. It has a lot of diferent places from big and technological cities to small and calm countryside.

Places to visit:

  • Tokyo's districts
    • Asakusa
    • Akihabara
    • Shinjuku
  • Kyoto
    • Kinkakuji
    • Nijo Castle
    • Fushimi-Inari Taisha Shrine
    • Kiyomizudera Temple
    • Gion's Geishas and Temples
  • Hokkaido

Tokyo has all types of places. It is famous for being the capital and the biggest city of Japan. Asakusa is a great place because of it's historical importance. Akihabara, on the other side, is known as the technological point of the city.

Kyoto has great historical places. There are numerous castles that where used by the Shoguns as Kyoto was the old capital.

Hokkaido is a colder place, great for winter sports. The 1972 Winter Olympics was held in Sapporo and the facilities built for it are still working and being visited by tourists
